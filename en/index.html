Biography

HU Zhicheng (胡志成) is an associate professor of computational mathematics at School of Mathematics, Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics, China. His research interests include efficient numerical methods, especially multigrid method, adaptive method, and moment method, for partial differential equations, and their applications in the fields such as fluid dynamics, kinetic theory.
